# Fenyu - App Store English Description

Fenyu is an emotion journaling app focused on two core actions: capture your feelings and review/export your reflection by week or month.  
You can turn your emotional timeline into a review image and save it to your photo library for personal reflection.

## Core Value

1. Emotion Logging: capture mood intensity, triggers, and current state with the Mood Weather flow.
2. Review and Export: review weekly/monthly changes, generate a reflection image, and save it to Photos.
3. Revisit and Reflect: look back through your release archive and action notes to build a consistent self-reflection habit.

## Core Scenarios

1. At the end of the day, quickly log how you felt so the moment is not lost.
2. During weekly or monthly review, see patterns in emotional ups and downs.
3. Save the review image to Photos for long-term personal tracking.

## Privacy and Boundaries

- Export behavior is "save to your photo library"; there is no built-in third-party sharing inside the app.  
- Emotional content may contain sensitive personal information. Please evaluate photo visibility and privacy risk before sharing from your library.  
- Data storage is local-first, with optional cloud sync.  
- The app may provide a gentle one-line summary as secondary support; if network/service is unavailable, fallback text is used and logging/export still works.  
- Fenyu is not a medical app and does not provide diagnosis or treatment advice.
